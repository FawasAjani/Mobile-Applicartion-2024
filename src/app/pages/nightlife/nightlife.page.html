Nightlife Nightlife in Roscommon Roscommon's nightlife is a vibrant blend of tradition and conviviality, attracting visitors with its cozy taverns and lively bars. Renowned for its warm hospitality and live music until around midnight, it's a must-visit for those seeking an authentic Irish experience. From traditional folk tunes to modern hits, the town's nightlife scene encapsulates the essence of Irish conviviality, making it a memorable destination for locals and travelers alike. jj harlows JJ Harlow’s, one of Roscommon’s oldest traditional bars, has a cozy and welcoming atmosphere.
